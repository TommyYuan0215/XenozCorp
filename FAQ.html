+ How do I sign up for an Online Customer Service (OCS) account?
To sign up, you must be a Xenoz customer with an active postpaid, prepaid, mobile broadband or home fibre account. To register, click here.
Just click on the "Register" tab and follow the simple instructions to complete your registration..


+ I forgot my OCS password. How can I retrieve it?
You can always recover your password in just 3 steps:

Step 1: Select ‘Forgot Username or Password?’ link in our OCS portal
Step 2: Type in your username or mobile number & ID number (NRIC/Old IC/Passport/Army/Police).
Step 3: Select “Submit” to proceed.

You will receive a temporary password by SMS or email (valid for 24 hours), which you can use to login. Do change your password to a new one of your choice.


+ What transactions can I do in OCS?
Below are some of the transaction you can do in OCS:

Postpaid –

View & Pay Bill
Bill Download
Change Billing Address
Subscribe Auto Billing
Subscribe Internet add-on and other Value-Added service
SIM replacement

Prepaid –

View Balance and Usage
Subscribe Internet add-on and other Value-Added Service
Reload
Validity Extension
SIM replacement


+ How do I pay my bill using OCS?
You can enjoy the flexibility of choice in online payment at OCS using:

Locally issued Credit / Debit Card
Online Banking via FPX


+ I have multiple Xenoz lines. Can I pay all my bills through one transaction at OCS?
Yes, you can pay all your Xenoz bills in one transaction. You can also make payment for other Xenoz numbers using the steps below:
Step 1: Select ‘View Account Summary’
Step 2: On the right side, select ‘Pay All bill’
Step 3: Select ‘Add Number Now’. Enter mobile number and amount to pay.
Step 4: Select ‘Proceed with payment’.

+ Does the Xenoz Xtra Prepaid Starter Pack comes with Freebies?

+ Where can I sign up for a new Xenoz Xtra Prepaid Starter Pack?
You may walk in to any Xenoz House and authorized Dealers. This pack is also available online with free delivery.


+ What do I need to know about the monthly freebies?*
- You will need to stay active to enjoy the freebies.

Xenoz Xtra Prepaid Internet Passes

+ What is Internet Pass?
Internet Pass offers customers with more quota to surf comes with validity of daily, weekly or monthly.


+ How do I subscribe and track my Internet Pass?
You can purchase any subscription services, track and manage your account via Xenoz Life App or by dialing *188#.


+ What is the difference between new Internet passes versus existing Internet Passes?
- The two new Internet passes RM12 | Unlimited | 7 Days and RM35 | Unlimited | 30 Days are available to all existing and new Xpax customer for a limited time offer only
- These NEW Internet passes have speed capped at 4Mbps
- These NEW Internet passes comes with Unlimited Calls and SMS to All Network
- These NEW Internet passes do not come with Unlimited Late Night YouTube and iflix VIP access


+ What do I need to know about the Hotspot Quota for the two new Internet passes?
- FREE 1GB Hotspot with Unlimited Internet RM12 Weekly and 3GB Hotspot with Unlimited Internet RM35 Monthly with speed capped at 4Mbps
- Hotspot service will be disabled once the free hotspot quota fully utilized
- Hotspot is enable for any Internet Pass without free hotspot quota and will be utilized from main Internet quota


+ What do I need to know about the Unlimited Calls to all network for the new Internet passes?
- FREE with Unlimited Internet RM12 Weekly and Unlimited Internet RM35 Monthly only
- The Unlimited Calls apply to all networks and for domestic usages only (i.e. within Malaysian networks) excluding video calls, calls to toll-free numbers or numbers with special charges (i.e. 1-300/1-800/600, 121, TM100, 1MOCC, IDD calls to Singapore & IDD/border calls to Brunei).


+ What is the usage priority for Internet passes?
- Internet Passes (high speed)
- Internet passes - Unlimited (3Mbps)
- Basic Internet (64kbps)
